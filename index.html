Sewage Free Seas 2016

Join Surfers Against Sewage in calling for an end to sewage pollution at our beaches.

Sewage free seas may finally be within reach.

Surfers Against Sewage (SAS) has been monitoring sewage pollution at hundreds of beaches and surf spots nationwide. Shockingly, some of the nationâ€™s best beaches can be polluted by raw sewage up to 60 times during the summer. This pollution could make you very sick and harms the marine environment we believe should be protected.

In March 2016, we have the best opportunity in a generation to stop sewage pollution at our beaches. And we need your support.

The government will be running a consultation on the regulation allowing water companies to discharge sewage pollution into our seas. The consultation is open for 6 weeks. During this period SAS and our partners are calling for 10,000 surfers, water users, boarders, swimmers and all beach lovers to join us in calling on the government for sewage free seas! In April 2016, we will deliver your message of support directly to the Environment Minister.

Together we can deliver a powerful message calling for Sewage Free Seas! #sewagefreeseas
